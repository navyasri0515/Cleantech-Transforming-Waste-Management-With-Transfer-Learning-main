CleanTech Waste Classifier

AI-powered litter recognition for smarter cities

Project Overview

CleanTech leverages transfer learning (VGG-16) to classify coastal waste images into Biodegradable, Recyclable, and Trash. Integrated with smart bins, drones, or city dashboards, the system automates sorting, cuts labor costs, and boosts recycling yields.

  • 🥇 Validation accuracy ≈ 80 %
  • ⚙️ Tech Stack: TensorFlow · Flask · Bootstrap 5
  • 🕒 Real-time inference < 120 ms / image
  • 🌍 Deployable to edge devices (Jetson Nano)

Key Features

🌊 Coastal Analytics

Heat maps reveal litter density, guiding clean-up crews to hotspots.

🚛 Fleet Routing

Live composition data routes trucks to compost, recycle, or landfill facilities.

📈 Sustainability Reports

Monthly dashboards quantify plastic vs. organic trends for policy and outreach.
